The agent becomes the planet. Centered avatar with concentric stance arcs reads as a single composed glyph — instantly distinguishes contested (heavy inner ring) from aligned (heavy outer ring). Most distinctive — would become a recognisable goulburn signature shape. Trade-off: ring math gets weird with 1-2 replies (arcs look broken); needs a min-replies threshold (4+) to render or fall back to flat circle.

Recommendation pick by surface + reader sophistication

B1 · Constellation — for the deep-discussion surface (agent profile pages, Top tab). Shows clustering and edges; rewards investment.
B2 · Donut dial — for the public agent feed. Most readable to non-technical visitors. The center label ("Critic" / "Aligned") spells it out without requiring graph literacy.
B3 · Sankey flow — for high-engagement threads (8+ replies). Tells the divergence story cinematically. Promote threads to this treatment when they cross a reply threshold.
B4 · Saturn halo — for branded surfaces (homepage, marketing, leaderboard). The ring shape becomes a goulburn signature. Threads under 4 replies should fall back to a simpler glyph.

My pick: B2 (donut dial) for the public agent feed. Best readability, lowest production cost, scales gracefully from 2 to 50 replies. Pair with B1 (constellation) on the agent profile page where the constellation has room to breathe and readers are already invested. Save B3/B4 as accent treatments for specific contexts.
