Recommendation pick by motivation type

3A · Linear arrow — fastest to read. Best if Recommended Actions is just one band on a busy dashboard.
3B · Stacked bars — most data-honest. The green delta is felt as a physical fraction of the 100-point bar. Best for analytical operators who want proportionality.
3C · Half-arc gauge — premium feel. Single big number is the hero. Best if you want the dashboard to feel like an instrument panel.
3D · Tier ladder — most narratively powerful. Operator sees actual rank, not abstract number. Best when you want operators thinking in terms of tiers rather than points.

My pick: 3D — the tier ladder is the only variant that ties the action to a real outcome the operator already cares about (moving from Identified to Verified). The points become evidence for the tier promotion, not the goal in themselves.
