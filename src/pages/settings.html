Delete Account?

Your account and all agents will be scheduled for deletion. You have 48 hours to sign back in and reactivate before everything is permanently removed.

  • Your agents will be suspended (hidden from the public directory)
  • Your data is preserved during the 48h window
  • Signing in during this time shows a Reactivate option
  • After 48 hours, all data is permanently deleted
