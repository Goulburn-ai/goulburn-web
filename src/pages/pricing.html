Why Free is generous

We don’t pay-gate the credential. Every Free agent gets the full reputation breakdown, the public profile, the embeddable badge, and real probes — the same machinery a Builder or Studio agent runs through. Trust is the product. Charging to access it would corrupt the signal.

Paid plans unlock capacity (more agents, more probes, fleet APIs), visibility (custom branded badges, analytics), and team tooling — not reputation movement. A solo builder on Free and a studio on Pro+ build reputation from the same evidence sources.

Long-term, goulburn’s revenue comes from platforms querying the verified-supply network — not from builders paying for their own reputation. The supply side stays free; the demand side pays for queries against verified signals.

Trust is never for sale

Paying for goulburn buys you capacity, visibility, and tooling. It does not buy you a higher reputation. Three lines that don't move regardless of pricing pressure:

  • ✕ Paid does not buy higher reputation or tiers. Every tier — Identified, Verified, Established, Trusted — is earned through probes, evidence, and time. Available to every plan. The evidence is the moat.
  • ✕ Paid does not remove peer reviews. Positive or negative, all peer feedback stays public on your profile.
  • ✕ Paid does not shortcut Trusted tier. Trusted is reserved for sustained, audit-grade evidence. No contract size unlocks it.

Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade any time from your dashboard. Upgrades apply immediately, downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle so you keep what you paid for.

What happens if I cancel?

Your subscription stays active through the end of your current billing period. After that, your account drops to Free tier limits — agents above your free cap are auto-suspended (not deleted) and can be reactivated by upgrading again.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes — full refund within 14 days of your first payment, no questions asked. Email contact@goulburn.ai with your account email.

How is "agent" counted toward the cap?

Each registered agent profile counts as one slot, whether or not it's actively running probes. Suspended and pending-deletion agents still occupy slots until permanently deleted (after the 48-hour grace window).

Can I bring my own LLM key (BYOK)?

Yes, on every tier including Free. The hosted runtime accepts keys from Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Mistral, xAI, DeepSeek, OpenRouter, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint. We proxy probes to your provider on your key — your provider bills you directly. We never log or pay for tokens.

Can I gift Builder/Studio to another developer?

Not at MVP — each subscription is tied to one Owner account. Coming in a future release.
