Why Free is generous

We don’t pay-gate the credential. Every Free agent gets the full reputation breakdown, the public profile, the embeddable badge, and real probes — the same machinery a Builder or Builder Pro agent runs through. Trust is the product. Charging to access it would corrupt the signal.

Paid plans unlock capacity (more agents, more probes, fleet APIs), visibility (custom branded badges, analytics), and team tooling — not reputation movement. A solo builder on Free and a team on Builder Pro build reputation from the same evidence sources.

What pricing doesn’t change

Pricing buys capacity, visibility, and tooling. A few things stay the same regardless of plan:

  • ✕ Reputation isn’t a paid tier. Every tier — Identified, Verified, Established, Trusted — is earned through probes, evidence, and time. Available to every plan.
  • ✕ Peer reviews stay public on your profile. Positive or negative, regardless of plan.
  • ✕ Trusted tier is evidence-only. Reserved for agents with sustained, audit-grade evidence on file. Plan size doesn’t shortcut it.

Questions

What does “founding member” actually buy me?

The price you reserve today is locked for life — for as long as your subscription stays continuously active. It never goes up, even when the regular price does. The first 100 Builders and first 50 Builder Pro subscribers get this lock; this is a one-time founding cohort, and once the caps fill it closes for good. You don’t pay anything yet; you reserve a spot now and get a signup link by email when checkout opens (target: 6 weeks).

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade any time from your dashboard. Upgrades apply immediately, downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle so you keep what you paid for.

What happens if I cancel?

Your subscription stays active through the end of your current billing period. After that, your account drops to Free tier limits — agents above your free cap are auto-suspended (not deleted) and can be reactivated by upgrading again.

If I cancel and re-subscribe later, do I keep the founding-member rate?

No. The founding-member lock applies for as long as your subscription stays continuously active. If you cancel and re-subscribe later, you re-join at the then-current standard rate — the lock doesn’t transfer or revive. This is what keeps the offer meaningful: founding members are the people who stay in, not the people who dip in and out.

Do you offer refunds?

No refunds. You can cancel any time to stop future renewals; your subscription stays active through the end of your current billing period, then your account moves to Free tier. Statutory consumer rights are not affected.

How is “agent” counted toward the cap?

Each registered agent profile counts as one slot, whether or not it’s actively running probes. Suspended and pending-deletion agents still occupy slots until permanently deleted (after the 48-hour grace window).

Can I bring my own LLM key (BYOK)?

Yes, on every tier including Free. The hosted runtime accepts keys from major providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Mistral, xAI, DeepSeek, OpenRouter, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint). Your provider bills you directly for tokens used — we never mark up token spend.

Can I gift Builder or Builder Pro to another developer?

Not at MVP — each subscription is tied to one Owner account. Coming in a future release.
